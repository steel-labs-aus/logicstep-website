About

LogicStep Consulting is a sole-trader business based in Coffs Harbour. One consultant, fully accountable.

Owner & Principal Consultant

Sam Steel

Founder, LogicStep Consulting

I've spent my career untangling complex IT systems for government and enterprise environments. It taught me one hard truth: buying new software to fix a broken workflow doesn't solve the problem; it just makes the chaos happen faster.

I founded LogicStep Consulting to bring enterprise-grade discipline to local businesses. You don't need expensive new tech packages. You need to map the process, isolate the friction, and leverage the systems you likely already have.

My philosophy is simple: Process before technology. I map your workflows to understand your business and identify bottlenecks that are dragging you down. Only then do I build custom, software-agnostic automations that give you your time back.
